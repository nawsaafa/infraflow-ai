## Investment Decision Framework

### GREEN LIGHT (Lowest Risk)
**Countries:** UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Botswana, Chile, Uruguay
- Investment grade ratings
- Low corruption (CPI >55)
- Stable political environment
- Strong institutional frameworks

### YELLOW LIGHT (Moderate Risk - Due Diligence Required)
**Countries:** Saudi Arabia, Morocco, South Africa, Brazil, Mexico, Peru, Colombia
- Near investment grade or upper speculative grade
- Moderate corruption (CPI 30-55)
- Reform momentum positive
- Sector-specific opportunities

### ORANGE LIGHT (Elevated Risk - Enhanced Monitoring)
**Countries:** Nigeria, Kenya, Tanzania, Jordan, Egypt, Paraguay, Guyana
- Mid-tier speculative grade
- Recent upgrades or positive outlook
- Higher corruption but improving
- Requires political risk insurance

### RED LIGHT (High Risk - Avoid or Extreme Caution)
**Countries:** Argentina, Ghana, Ethiopia, Zambia, Lebanon, Venezuela
- Default or near-default
- Very high corruption
- Political instability
- Significant capital controls

## Quick Reference: Rating Scales

### OECD Country Risk (0-7)
- 0-1: Negligible risk
- 2-3: Low risk
- 4-5: Moderate risk
- 6-7: High risk

### Corruption Perceptions Index (0-100)
- 70-100: Very clean
- 50-69: Clean
- 30-49: Corrupt
- 10-29: Very corrupt
- 0-9: Extremely corrupt

### Credit Ratings

**Investment Grade:**
- Moody's: Aaa, Aa1-Aa3, A1-A3, Baa1-Baa3
- S&P/Fitch: AAA, AA+/AA/AA-, A+/A/A-, BBB+/BBB/BBB-

**Speculative Grade:**
- Moody's: Ba1-Ba3, B1-B3, Caa1-Caa3, Ca, C
- S&P/Fitch: BB+/BB/BB-, B+/B/B-, CCC+/CCC/CCC-, CC, C, D
